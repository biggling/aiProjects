## Scoring Criteria
Each project scored 1–5 on:
- **Market Size** — addressable market scale
- **Competition** — gap vs. saturation (5 = low competition / clear gap)
- **Effort** — time/skill required (5 = low effort for BiG's profile)
- **Speed to Revenue** — time from start to first dollar
- **Passive Potential** — recurring income without daily work

**Overall Score** = weighted average. BiG has <5 hrs/week — effort and speed are weighted 2x.
