## 1. Play Store Productivity Apps — Benchmarks
- **Market Growth:** Global productivity app revenue is projected to hit $14.46B in 2026.
- **Winning Models:** Subscription remains dominant (e.g., Freedom at $39.99/yr), but gamified apps like Forest lead in retention.
- **AI Integration:** 32% of new features in 2026 focus on "Agentic AI"—apps that perform tasks (scheduling, drafting) rather than just tracking them.

## 2. AdMob Revenue Benchmarks (Thailand)
- **eCPM Rates:** 
  - Rewarded Video: $2.20 – $3.85 (Highest potential for "Emergency Break" features).
  - Interstitial: $1.65 – $3.10 (Best for task-completion triggers).
  - Banner: $0.09 – $0.20 (Generally not recommended for productivity).
- **Volume Needed:** For $100/month, aim for ~20,000 DAU with 2.5 impressions per session.

## 3. Competitor Analysis — Focus/Block Apps
- **Major Complaints:** Low bypass resistance (users just turn off VPN or force stop), high subscription costs, and "barebones" Android versions compared to iOS.
- **Gap:** A "Hard Block" mode that requires a physical trigger (NFC) or an "Unbreakable" system-level lock to prevent self-sabotage.
- **Opportunity:** A one-time purchase or "pay-per-break" model to disrupt the $100/year subscription fatigue.

## 4. Kotlin + Jetpack Compose Updates
- **Pausable Composition:** Compose 1.10+ allows skipping work if the frame budget is exceeded, vital for long task lists.
- **Android 16 Restrictions:** Background network access is now immediately cut 5 seconds after app minimization unless a Foreground Service is active.
- **FGS Timeouts:** Data-sync services are killed after 6 hours, requiring `WorkManager` for all-day focus tracking.

## 5. Thai Android Market
- **The AI Gap:** Thais are heavy AI users but struggle to apply it to complex workflows.
- **LINE Integration:** LINE is the de-facto OS. Apps that integrate with LINE Notify or use LINE-style UI patterns perform better.
- **Language:** "Agentic Localization" (understanding Thai social nuances) is the 2026 standard for high-rating apps.
