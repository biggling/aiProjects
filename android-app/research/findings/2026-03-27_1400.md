## Play Store Gamification Trends

- **Streaks are the #1 retention mechanic**: 7-day streaks produce 3.6x higher long-term retention (Duolingo/SensorTower data) — https://sensortower.com/blog/duolingo-streak-feature-app-engagement-growth
- **Streak Freeze = dual-value mechanic**: Watching a rewarded ad to protect a breaking streak reduced churn 21% for at-risk users; streak wager boosts Day-14 retention by 14% — https://www.orizon.co/blog/duolingos-gamification-secrets
- **Streak push notifications** boost engagement 25% when streak is at risk of breaking — https://enable3.io/blog/mobile-app-retention-2025
- **Badges** lift task completion rates 30%; XP leaderboards drive 40% more engagement but risk demotivating solo users — use opt-in friend comparison only — https://beedone.co/en/blog/best-gamified-productivity-apps-2026/
- **2026 trend**: "Intrinsic-first" design (habit rings, streaks, progress maps) is winning over gamey points/XP in productivity categories
- **Headspace case study**: Daily streaks + progress milestones drove 35% increase in weekly active users in 2025
