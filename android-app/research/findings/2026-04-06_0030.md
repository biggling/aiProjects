# android-app Research — 2026-04-06_0030

## 1. Play Store Productivity Benchmarks (2026)
- **Revenue Shift:** 98% of Google Play revenue comes from free-to-download apps (IAP/Ads). ARPU for productivity is ~$6.19.
- **Discovery Baseline:** A 4.5+ star rating is required for Play Store algorithm visibility; 71% of users churn within 90 days.
- **Trend:** "Agentic AI" (apps that do the work, not just track it) is the standard.
- **Source:** [Tekrevol/AppVerticals 2026], [BusinessOfApps 2026]

## 2. AdMob Revenue Benchmarks (Thailand)
- **eCPM Rates:** Rewarded ($4.00–$6.50), Interstitial ($3.50–$5.00), Banner ($0.25–$0.60).
- **DAU Target:** ~530–600 DAU in Thailand needed to earn $100/mo with a 1.5 ads/user frequency.
- **Seasonality:** Q4 eCPMs are 30-50% higher; Q1 (Jan) usually sees a 20% reset.
- **Source:** [AnyMind Group 2026], [Market Analysis]

## 3. Competitor Analysis — Focus/Block Apps
- **Hard Block Trend:** Apps like **Blok** (NFC-based) and **AppBlock** (Strict Mode) are winning.
- **The "Forest" Weakness:** Users complain Forest is "too easy to cheat." 1-star reviews cite "soft" blocks that don't stop scrolling.
- **Friction-as-a-Feature:** **ScreenZen** and **One Sec** (introducing delays/breathing before opening apps) are highly rated for habit-breaking.
- **Source:** [Reddit r/productivity], [YouTube: Best 2026 Focus Apps]

## 4. Kotlin + Jetpack Compose / Android 16 Updates
- **API 36 (Android 16):** Mandatory edge-to-edge display and adaptive layouts for large screens (locks on orientation are ignored).
- **FGS Restriction:** Foreground Services now limited to 6 hours/day. Apps must use `JobScheduler` for long-term monitoring.
- **Compose 1.9:** Stable `dropShadow()`, `PredictiveBackHandler`, and performance tracking modifiers are now standard.
- **Source:** [Android Developers Blog April 2026]

## 5. Thai Android Market
- **Payment Maturity:** **K PLUS** (KASIKORNBANK) is now a direct Play Store payment method.
- **AI Nuance Gap:** Global tools fail at Thai business etiquette and honorifics. Localized "Cultural Fluency" is a major opportunity.
- **Super App Dominance:** LINE and Grab are command centers; standalone apps need LINE integration or very specific niche utility to survive.
- **Source:** [NECTEC], [StatCounter Thailand 2026]
