# android-app Research — 2026-04-06_0300

## Play Store Productivity Benchmarks (2026)
- **Monetization Reality**: 98% of revenue comes from freemium/subscriptions. One-time purchases are nearly extinct in the top charts. [Source: Apptunix/Apploye 2026]
- **Visibility Threshold**: A 4.5+ star rating is the new baseline for Play Store algorithmic visibility. Apps with <15% 90-day retention are deprioritized. [Source: IPHTechnologies]
- **AI Agent Shift**: The market has moved from "chatbots" to "autonomous agents." Productivity apps that expose capabilities via the new **Android 16 AppFunctions SDK** are getting featured placement. [Source: Google Blog 2026]

## AdMob Revenue Benchmarks (Thailand Focus)
- **Thailand eCPM Projections**: Interstitial ($1.50–$2.20), Rewarded ($2.50–$4.50), Banner ($0.15–$0.40). [Source: MonetizeMore]
- **Target DAU for $100/mo**: In Thailand, with a conservative ARPDAU of $0.005–$0.006, you need approximately **530–600 Daily Active Users (DAU)** to reach $100/month in ad revenue.
- **US Arbitrage**: US eCPMs are 6–8x higher than Thailand. A hybrid strategy targeting Thai users for volume and US users for revenue is recommended.

## Competitor Pain Points — The "Gap"
- **Forest**: Users are revolting against the "Premium Ultimate" subscription model and "forced" features like mandatory breathing exercises that can't be skipped easily.
- **Freedom**: The biggest complaint is **battery drain (10-15%)** caused by its local VPN blocking method.
- **Focus To-Do**: Users find the UI "clunky and ugly" and hate the restrictive 25-minute fixed timer in the free version.
- **Opportunity**: A "Surgical/Hard" blocker that doesn't use a VPN (to save battery) and offers a clean, modern Material 3 Expressive UI without aggressive subscription prompts.

## Android 16 (API 36) Technical Hurdles
- **Accessibility Lockdown**: The new `accessibilityDataSensitive` flag allows apps to hide their UI from accessibility services. This breaks traditional "screen-reading" blockers.
- **isAccessibilityTool Flag**: Google Play now strictly reviews apps using this flag. If the app isn't explicitly for users with disabilities, it risks rejection.
- **Edge-to-Edge Mandatory**: Apps can no longer opt-out. Content must be drawn behind system bars using `WindowInsets`. [Source: Android Dev Blog]
- **Large Screen Flexibility**: Android 16 ignores app-defined orientation locks on screens ≥600dp (tablets/foldables).

- **PromptPay Integration**: A productivity gap exists for tools that track micro-tasks or expenses and integrate with Thai QR payment workflows.
- **Work-Life Architecture**: Thai users prefer tools that manage both personal (family/errands) and work tasks in one "dashboard" view. [Source: Hyperwork Recruitment 2026]
