## Business Model

**TikTok Affiliate Marketing + Content Automation**
- Create high-engagement short videos (15–60s)
- Embed TikTok affiliate product links in bio and video captions
- Drive traffic to TikTok Shop / Amazon affiliate / product landing pages
- Secondary revenue: brand sponsorships (once 10k+ followers)
- Target: $3,000+/month from affiliate commissions + sponsored posts

## Manual Step-by-Step Process

### Area 1: Niche & Account Setup
1. Research 3–5 profitable niches: beauty, fitness, gadgets, food, pets
2. Create TikTok account with keyword-rich bio
3. Link affiliate account (TikTok Shop, Amazon, ShareASale)
4. Define posting schedule: 2–3 videos/day optimal
5. Set up analytics tracking (TikTok Business Center)

### Area 2: Content Research
1. Open TikTok → search niche keywords → filter "This week"
2. Identify top 5 videos by view count in niche
3. Analyze: hook style, video length, editing pace, music used
4. Check competitor hashtags (top 5 competitors)
5. Note trending sounds/music for the week
6. Research trending products in TikTok Shop for niche

### Area 3: Script & Content Creation
1. Write hook (0–3s): bold claim or curiosity gap statement
2. Write body: show product/tip in action (demonstrate value)
3. Write CTA: "Link in bio" or "Comment [keyword] for link"
4. Generate voiceover script (conversational, 2nd person)
5. Select trending sound from week's research
6. Create or source visual assets (product footage, B-roll)

### Area 4: Video Production
1. Record or generate video content (15–30s for best retention)
2. Edit: cut on beat, add text overlays, captions, transitions
3. Add trending sound at 15–20% volume under voiceover
4. Add branded intro/outro (0.5s each)
5. Add on-screen text (hook text + CTA text)
6. Export: 1080×1920, H.264, 30fps

### Area 5: Posting & Hashtags
1. Upload video at optimal time: 7–9 AM, 12–3 PM, 7–9 PM (local)
2. Caption: hook headline + 3–5 hashtags (1 broad, 2 niche, 1 trending)
3. Add product link sticker (TikTok Shop) or bio link
4. Select cover frame (high-energy moment)
5. Set video as "Public" and enable "Allow duet/stitch"

### Area 6: Engagement & Growth
1. Reply to all comments within first 30 minutes (boosts algorithm)
2. Stitch/duet top competitors' videos with reaction
3. Follow 20–30 accounts in niche per day (follow-for-follow)
4. Like + comment on trending videos in niche (appear in comment section)
5. Pin best-performing video to profile

### Area 7: Affiliate & Monetization
1. Check weekly: which products got most clicks (TikTok analytics)
2. Create follow-up video for top-performing products
3. Negotiate brand deals (DM brands with 5k+ followers)
4. Set up email capture via landing page (Linktree or Beacons)
5. Track commissions: TikTok Shop dashboard, Amazon Associates

### Area 8: Analytics & Optimization
1. Check daily: views, likes, shares, follows gained
2. Identify videos that hit >1000 views in 24h → replicate
3. Identify videos below 200 views after 48h → analyze why + retry
4. A/B test: same topic, different hook → compare performance
5. Monthly: analyze top 10 videos, document what worked
