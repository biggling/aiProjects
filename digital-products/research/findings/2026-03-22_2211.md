## Bundle & Pricing Strategy

- **20-40% bundle discount** vs. individual prices is the sweet spot
- Tiered pricing (Basic $29 / Pro $49 / Premium $99): ~70% of buyers choose mid-tier
- Anchor pricing: adding a $97 upsell makes $29 feel cheap even if nobody buys the $97
- **PWYW launch strategy**: 46% conversion rate (vs. ~7% fixed price); 20% of PWYW buyers voluntarily pay above suggested; 20.8% of PWYW products reach 10+ ratings vs. 8.0% fixed
- Reviews from paying customers only — PWYW at $0 still counts (if customer pays any amount)
- Gumroad minimum payment: $0.99 if customer chooses to pay on PWYW product
- Launch at $0 PWYW → collect reviews → switch to fixed price ($27-$37) after 10-20 reviews
- Gumroad does NOT refund its 10% fee on refunds — specificity reduces refunds
