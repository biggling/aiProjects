## Product Format Intelligence

- **ZIP bundles of ready-to-use files beat PDF guides** for developer buyers: they want to drop files in and start, not read.
- **Bundles convert better than single files**: curated collections outperform individual downloads on Gumroad.
- **Notion templates** work well for process/workflow products (PM workflows, prompt libraries).
- **Buyers pay for time savings, not information**: "saves X hours of setup" framing outperforms feature lists.
- **One-time price preferred** over subscription — reduces anxiety for technical buyers.
- **Token conservation is table stakes** — include as bonus chapter, not standalone product.
