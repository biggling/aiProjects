# micro-saas Research — 2026-03-27_2030

## Shopee Platform & Tool Ecosystem

- **Shopee Penalty Points (AI-Automated)**: Seller Performance Policy v4.2 (March 2026) uses AI to monitor fulfillment latency, return reason accuracy, chat response times, and image metadata compliance. 83% of point deductions now come from algorithmic flagging (not customer reports). A 12-minute delay in first chat response during peak hours can trigger a 5-point deduction — enough to suspend a new account within 72 hours. Penalties trigger automatically within 90 minutes of confirmed breach. [https://www.alibaba.com/product-insights/shopee-penalty-points-system-explained-2026.html]
- **No real-time penalty alert tool exists**: Shopdora, Feexivo, and other existing tools cover analytics/fee calculation but not live policy breach risk monitoring. [https://blog.shopdora.com/en/page/best-shopee-analytics-tools-in-2026-complete-guide-comparison/]
- **Shopee 5% Technical Fee Correction**: The 5% Technical Support Fee was effective February 1, 2026 (not April 1). Applies to SG, MY, TH, VN for all seller types including cross-border. [https://www.bigseller.com/blog/articleDetails/4190/null.htm]
- **Shopee April 2026 Activity**: 4.4 Mega Sale (April 4) is the dominant event; no new policy/fee changes found specifically for April 1. [https://blog.shopdora.com/en/page/march-23-2026-shopee-vietnam-market-weekly-report-prepping-for-the-4-4-mega-sale/]
- **3-Month Commission-Free for New Stores**: Launched Jan 1, 2026; covers SG, MY, TH, VN, PH, TW; capped at 500 orders/month. [https://www.forestshipping.com/shopee-2026-commission-free-new-stores]
- **Product Identity Verification (PIV)**: Mandatory for new listings in electronics, cosmetics, baby products, and branded apparel. [https://seller.shopee.sg/edu/article/26463]

## TikTok Shop Attribute Compliance Tools

- **No third-party enrichment tool exists**: Post March 20, 2026 mandatory attribute lockdown, no dedicated SaaS fills required product fields (material composition, manufacturer, safety standards) automatically. CJ Dropshipping's API sync helps CJ users only; everyone else does it manually. [https://cjdropshipping.com/blogs/business-insights/TikTok-Shop-s-March-20-Deadline--Navigating-the-Mandatory-Attribute-Lockdown]
- **AI Frame Analysis enforcement**: TikTok's AI cross-checks video claims against product data fields and suppresses listings with inconsistencies. "Location Spoofing" (ship-from vs. carrier first scan mismatch) = Tier-3 violation, 7-day store suspension. [https://www.shoplazza.com/blog/tiktok-shop-policy-update]
- **Gap**: An AI-powered bulk attribute enrichment tool (upload CSV → auto-fill missing fields via product name + images + public databases) would directly address the #1 post-March 20 seller pain point. [Synthesis]

## Webhook Tools Landscape

- **Hookdeck** is the dominant inbound webhook tool; expanded to "Outpost" (outbound) in 2025. Free tier: 100K req/mo; $39/mo for 1M. [https://hookdeck.com/webhooks/platforms/best-webhook-management-platforms]
- **Svix pricing cliff**: $0 → $490/mo with no mid-tier option — creates clear gap for teams doing 100K–500K messages/month. [https://www.svix.com/pricing/]
- **Convoy**: MIT-licensed, self-hosted, handles both inbound and outbound on PostgreSQL. [https://magicbell.com/blog/best-open-source-webhook-services]
- **LeanMQ**: New open-source Redis-based webhook replacement with DLQ, transactions, TTL support. [https://dev.to/augiwan/i-open-sourced-our-redis-based-webhook-replacement-and-why-you-should-try-it-1lo3]
- **Webhookify**: Webhook.site alternative with persistent endpoints and AI-powered notifications. [https://webhookify.app/alternatives/webhook-site-alternatives]
