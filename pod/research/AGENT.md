# pod — Research Agent

## Context
Etsy print-on-demand (POD) store. Selling physical printed goods (t-shirts, mugs, posters, tote bags).
Using Printify or Printful for fulfillment. Target: passive income from evergreen niche designs.

## Research Tasks

### 1. Trending Design Themes on Etsy
Search: "Etsy trending designs 2026", "Etsy bestselling t-shirt niches 2026", "print on demand trending 2026"
- What design themes are currently trending (humor, cottage-core, dark academia, retro, etc.)?
- What niche communities are buying heavily right now (gamers, dog owners, nurses, teachers)?
- Any seasonal trends coming up in the next 45 days worth prepping for?

### 2. Competitor Shop Analysis
Search: "Etsy POD shop revenue 2026", "print on demand Etsy success 2026", "best selling POD designs Etsy"
- What do top-earning POD Etsy shops have in common (volume, niche, product type)?
- What are the top-selling product types (t-shirts still #1, or mugs/posters overtaking)?
- Price points for bestselling listings?

### 3. Design Gap Opportunities
Search: "Etsy underserved niche 2026", "low competition print on demand niche", "Etsy saturation check"
- Which niches show high search volume but few listings (opportunity)?
- Any emerging micro-niches before they get saturated?
- Thai/Southeast Asian culture design niches — is there demand in English-speaking markets?

### 4. Printify / Printful Updates
Search: "Printify new products 2026", "Printful product catalog 2026", "POD supplier comparison 2026"
- Any new product types available on Printify that competitors haven't saturated yet?
- Price changes affecting margin calculations?
- Any quality issues reported for specific products?

### 5. Etsy Algorithm & SEO
Search: "Etsy SEO 2026 changes", "Etsy listing optimization tips", "Etsy algorithm update 2026"
- Any Etsy algorithm changes affecting organic discovery?
- What tags/titles are driving traffic for POD shops?
- Does Etsy Ads pay off for POD at current CPCs?
