## 4. Etsy AI-Generated Designs Policy 2026

- **AI art is allowed**, but seller must play a clear creative role and be transparent. [etsy.com](https://www.etsy.com/seller-handbook/article/1275449912004)
- **Disclosure required:** Listing description must state AI was used. Cannot select "I made it" — must use "Designed by" in the About This Item section. [etsy.com](https://www.etsy.com/legal/creativity/)
- **Original prompts required:** Seller must write original prompts; purchased or templated prompts are not permitted. Cannot re-sell AI prompts. [ecombalance.com](https://ecombalance.com/ai-content-policies-2026/)
- **Prohibited:** Passing off AI output as handmade or original without disclosure; using licensed template prompts. [aicashcaptain.com](https://aicashcaptain.com/selling-ai-art-copyright-rules-2026-update/)
- **Policy is evolving:** Etsy explicitly states it will "periodically reevaluate" as AI capabilities advance. Sellers should monitor announcements. [etsy.com](https://www.etsy.com/seller-handbook/article/1275449912004)

## 5. POD Profit Margins 2026

Gross margin = selling price minus production cost. Net margin = after Etsy fees (~6.5% transaction + 3% + $0.25 payment processing + $0.20 listing).

| Product | Production Cost | Avg. Sell Price | Gross Margin | Net Margin |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Classic T-Shirt | $8–$12 | $25–$40 ($34.98 sample) | 50–65% | ~27% |
| Ceramic Mug | $4–$7 | $12–$20 ($29.98 sample) | 40–60% | ~24% |
| Personalized Sweatshirt | ~$20–$25 | $45–$60 ($50.98 sample) | 45–55% | ~31% |
| Tote Bag | ~$6–$10 | $18–$28 | ~50% | ~25–30% |
| Poster/Print | ~$4–$8 | $15–$25 | ~50–60% | ~25–30% |

- **Target net margin band:** 25–35% is "healthy and scalable." Under 15% is fragile. Over 35% typically requires premium or personalized products. [mydesigns.io](https://mydesigns.io/blog/print-on-demand-profit-margin/)
- **Personalized products beat commodity:** Personalized listings command higher prices and convert better, pushing net margins above 30%. [mydesigns.io](https://mydesigns.io/blog/print-on-demand-profit-margin/)
- **No economies of scale in POD:** Per-unit cost stays fixed regardless of volume unless on Printify Premium ($39/mo, up to 33% discount) or Printful Growth ($24.99/mo, up to 33% discount). [printify.com](https://printify.com/blog/most-profitable-print-on-demand-products/)
- **High-margin standouts in 2026:** Tote bags (flat surface, low production cost, year-round demand), stickers, and spiral notebooks are flagged as 50–80% gross margin products. [trueprofit.io](https://trueprofit.io/blog/high-margin-print-on-demand-products)
- **Mugs and phone cases** are more margin-compressed; tighter window between production cost and acceptable market price. [ippei.com](https://ippei.com/print-on-demand-profit-margins/)
