## Etsy SEO Updates

- **Mobile-first ranking:** 46% of Etsy sales now via mobile app. First photo must communicate product at a glance. Most buyers skip descriptions — use photo carousel for key info. [blog.marmalead.com](https://blog.marmalead.com/etsy-algorithm-2026/)
- **Engagement over keywords in 2026:** Keywords determine eligibility; clicks, favorites, add-to-cart, and purchase rate determine ranking position. CTR optimization is now the highest-ROI lever. [blog.marmalead.com](https://blog.marmalead.com/etsy-algorithm-2026/)
- **Attributes = extra tags:** Fill every relevant attribute — gives Etsy structured relevance data alongside tags/categories. Consistency across all three improves indexing. [mydesigns.io](https://mydesigns.io/blog/etsy-seo-guide-2026/)
- **Title format:** Primary product phrase in first 3–5 words. Format: `Primary product + descriptor + use case + gift angle`. Only ~50–60 chars display in search. [etsy.com/seller-handbook](https://www.etsy.com/seller-handbook/article/1399426136697)
- **Tags cover intent gaps only:** Don't repeat title wording in tags. Use 13 tags for occasion, audience, style, synonyms. [mydesigns.io](https://mydesigns.io/blog/etsy-seo-guide-2026/)
- **Shop-level quality matters:** Reviews, on-time fulfillment, and cancellation rate affect all listings' visibility. [blog.marmalead.com](https://blog.marmalead.com/etsy-algorithm-2026/)

## Etsy AI Policy (2026)

- **AI designs permitted** if seller plays a creative role with original prompts. Mandatory: disclosure in listing + "Designed by" (not "I made it"). [etsy.com](https://www.etsy.com/seller-handbook/article/1275449912004)
- **Banned:** Purchased/templated prompts, claiming AI output is handmade, selling AI prompts as products. [ecombalance.com](https://ecombalance.com/ai-content-policies-2026/)
