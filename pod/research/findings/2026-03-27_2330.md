## Topic 4: Etsy SEO Tips 2026

- **Title format — clarity first, 3–5 words upfront:** Lead with the clearest primary product phrase. Format: `Primary product + descriptor + use case/audience + gift angle`. Search engines display only first 50–60 characters; mobile truncates even earlier. Etsy's algorithm now penalizes keyword-stuffed titles. [etsy.com/seller-handbook](https://www.etsy.com/seller-handbook/article/1399426136697)
- **Tags cover intent, not title repeats:** Use all 13 tag slots, but don't repeat phrasing from your title. Tags should expand coverage: occasion-based ("anniversary present"), audience-based ("gift for mom"), style-based ("cottagecore"), and synonym variations. [mydesigns.io](https://mydesigns.io/blog/etsy-seo-guide-2026/)
- **Attributes act as extra tags:** Fill every relevant attribute field. Attributes function as structured relevance signals alongside tags and categories. Consistency between category, attributes, and tags improves indexing accuracy. [mydesigns.io](https://mydesigns.io/blog/etsy-seo-guide-2026/)
- **Engagement signals now outrank pure keyword matching:** Etsy's 2026 algorithm: keywords determine eligibility to appear; clicks, favorites, add-to-cart, and purchases determine ranking position. Listings with strong conversion rates travel further in search. [blog.marmalead.com](https://blog.marmalead.com/etsy-algorithm-2026/)
- **46% of Etsy sales now via mobile app:** Algorithm has shifted toward mobile-optimized listings. First photo must communicate the product at a glance; key info should be in the photo carousel since most buyers skip descriptions. [blog.marmalead.com](https://blog.marmalead.com/etsy-algorithm-2026/)
- **Shop-level quality score affects all listings:** Reviews, on-time fulfillment rate, and cancellation rate now affect catalog-wide visibility — not just individual listing performance. [blog.marmalead.com](https://blog.marmalead.com/etsy-algorithm-2026/)
- **Optimize near-miss listings first:** Listings with impressions but weak CTR/conversion are the highest-ROI SEO target — they're already indexed, just underperforming. Batch-test changes across 10–20 similar listings. [mydesigns.io](https://mydesigns.io/blog/etsy-seo-guide-2026/)
