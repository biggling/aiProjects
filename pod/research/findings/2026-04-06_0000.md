# pod Research — 2026-04-06_0000

## Etsy Algorithm & SEO Updates (April 2026)
- **Mobile-First Title Soft Cap:** Etsy now enforces a 70-character "soft cap" for listing titles to optimize for mobile app display (~46% of purchases). [listybox.com]
- **Semantic Intent (NLP):** Search has shifted from keyword matching to AI-powered intent interpretation. Conversational, descriptive titles (e.g., "Personalized Rustic Leather Journal for Writers") rank higher than keyword-stuffed strings. [s27pod.com]
- **Engagement-Based Ranking:** Real-time metrics (clicks, favorites, and "dwell time") are primary ranking signals. Video is now mandatory for high search placement as it autoplays on mobile to boost dwell time. [listybox.com]
- **Full History Shop Ratings:** As of April 1, 2026, shop ratings reflect the full history of the shop rather than just the last 3 months. [listybox.com]
- **Shipping Price Threshold:** Listings with shipping costs exceeding $6 (for US-based sellers) face reduced visibility in search. [listybox.com]

## Summer 2026 & Seasonal Trends

## Target Persona Comparison 2026
- **Gen Z (Social Shoppers):** Driven by TikTok/IG trends. Value "Main Character" energy and sustainability. Hardest to retain but fastest to go viral. [convergehub.com]
- **Millennials (Convenience Kings):** Highest online shopping frequency. Prioritize longevity, mobile-first checkout (Apple Pay/BNPL), and quality. [retailtouchpoints.com]
- **Gen X (Big Spenders):** Highest spending power. Do the most research and value high-utility, high-value items. Best opportunity for high-margin "investment" POD goods (blankets, leather items). [loopreturns.com]
