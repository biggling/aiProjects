- **Midnight Garden:** 2026 Color of the Year (deep muted green). Pairs with **Patina Blue** (Etsy Color of the Year) and **Washed Linen** (Texture of the Year).

## 5. Etsy SEO & Algorithm Updates (2026)
- **Semantic Search (NLP):** Algorithm matches *intent* over exact keywords. "Meaning" relevance is the first gatekeeper.
- **Title Optimization:** Prioritize the first 40 characters for mobile. Use natural, conversational language; avoid "keyword stuffing" (robot-speak).
- **Description Indexing:** Descriptions are now indexed by the NLP engine to provide context for matching.
- **Gift Mode:** Highly optimized for "Gift Mode" discovery; items must clearly define the "who" and "why" in descriptions.
