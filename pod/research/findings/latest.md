## Gelato vs Printify vs Printful 2026

- **Gelato: recommended default for new sellers.** Free plan (no monthly fee); 140+ facilities in 32 countries; algorithmic routing (no manual provider management); 90% local production = no customs fees; 3-5 day delivery. Base cost for Gildan 64000 to US: $6.96 + $4.54 shipping = $11.50 total (vs Printful $13.54 / Printify ~$14). [growingyourcraft.com](https://www.growingyourcraft.com/blog/gelato-vs-printful-vs-printify-comparison-for-international-etsy-sellers)
- **Printify: best for US-focused sellers at scale.** Largest catalog (900+ products). 15–20% lower base cost than Printful but requires manual print provider management. Higher return variability. Annual Premium plan ($299/yr) needed for 33% discounts. [growingyourcraft.com](https://www.growingyourcraft.com/blog/gelato-vs-printful-vs-printify-comparison-for-international-etsy-sellers)
- **Printful: best for premium brand positioning only.** Highest base costs; 99.5% order accuracy; 98% color accuracy; own fulfillment centers. Only viable if margin is protected by premium pricing or Growth plan ($24.99/mo). [podbase.com](https://www.podbase.com/blogs/gelato-vs-printful)
- **Recommended sequence:** Gelato first → add Printify Premium at 10+ monthly sales → Printful only for a deliberate premium brand tier. [printondemandbusiness.com](https://www.printondemandbusiness.com/blog/printful-vs-printify-vs-gelato/)

## Etsy Listing Photos & Video Requirements 2026

- **Images:** Up to 10 per listing; minimum 5 recommended. Recommended: 3000×2250px (4:3 ratio), landscape or square. Min: 2000px on shortest side. File types: JPG/GIF/PNG/SVG/HEIC. Under 1MB per image. sRGB color mode. [soona.co](https://soona.co/image-resizer/etsy-image-size-specs)
- **Video:** 5–15 seconds; minimum 1080p; MP4 format; 1 per listing; audio auto-removed. Phone-filmed beats studio mockup for authenticity in 2026. [insightagent.app](https://www.insightagent.app/guides/etsy-listing-video-requirements-size)
- **Photo sequence best practice:** (1) lifestyle hero mockup, (2) design detail close-up, (3) scale reference, (4) variant options, (5) personalization example, (6) text infographic (size guide/material/gifting callout). [listybox.com](https://listybox.com/blog/etsy-product-photography-guide-2026)
- **AI lifestyle mockups** now accepted and recommended — tools like Pebblely/Placeit eliminate generic stock image problem that suppresses CTR. [soona.co](https://soona.co/image-resizer/etsy-image-size-specs)
- **Mobile-first:** 46% of Etsy sales are mobile. First photo must communicate the product as a thumbnail. Most buyers skip description — put key info in the photo carousel. [blog.marmalead.com](https://blog.marmalead.com/etsy-algorithm-2026/)
