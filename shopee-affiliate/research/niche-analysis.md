## Recommended Niches

### 1. Beauty & Skincare (TOP PICK)
- **Why:** Highest volume, good commission, easy content (before/after, swatches, reviews)
- **Content format:** Product reviews, "สินค้าน่าซื้อ" (must-buy) lists, skincare routine videos
- **Audience:** Women 18-35, massive Thai Facebook/TikTok audience
- **Competition:** High but market is big enough

### 2. Home & Kitchen Gadgets
- **Why:** Viral demo potential, impulse purchases, good commission
- **Content format:** "ของใช้เด็ด" (amazing products) demo videos, before/after cleaning
- **Audience:** Women 25-45, homeowners
- **Competition:** Medium

### 3. Health Supplements
- **Why:** Highest commission rates, repeat purchases, growing market
- **Content format:** Educational content, reviews, comparison posts
- **Audience:** Health-conscious 25-50
- **Competition:** Medium, requires trust-building

## Successful Thai Shopee Affiliate Patterns
1. **Deal alert format**: "ลดราคา XX% วันนี้เท่านั้น!" — urgency-driven
2. **Comparison posts**: "เทียบ 3 ตัวเลือก ราคาไม่เกิน 500 บาท"
3. **Haul/unboxing**: Show multiple products, link each in comments
4. **Mega-sale countdowns**: Pre-sale content 1-2 weeks before X.X sales
5. **Repost & reshare**: Amplify viral product videos with affiliate links
