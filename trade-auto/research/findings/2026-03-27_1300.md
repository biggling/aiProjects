## Risk Management Research
- **Kelly Criterion — 2026 Consensus:** Fractional Kelly 25–50% of full Kelly confirmed as optimal for crypto. Half Kelly retains 75% of max growth rate while reducing variance to 25% of full Kelly volatility. For high-volatility assets (crypto, microcaps), quarter Kelly or less is advised. [medium.com/@tmapendembe_28659]
- **Drawdown Thresholds:** Circuit breakers pausing trading when losses exceed predefined limits are now considered essential, not optional. Real-time drawdown monitoring with hard stops is the 2026 standard. [darkbot.io]
- **Volatility-Adjusted Sizing:** Using ATR or Bollinger Band width to dynamically scale position size — reduce during high-volatility periods, increase when markets stabilize. Prevents over-sizing into the current high-fear environment. [darkbot.io, bitget.com]
- **Capital Reserve:** 70–80% long-term hold + 20–30% active trading allocation from r/algotrading community. Consistent with the 25% reserve guideline already in use. [azbigmedia.com]

Strategy-specific Kelly should be recalculated whenever the market regime shifts (e.g., from trending to sideways). Current regime (extreme fear + geopolitical + options expiry) warrants moving toward the 0.25x end of the 0.25–0.5x range. [medium.com/@tmapendembe_28659]
